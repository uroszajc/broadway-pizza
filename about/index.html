From Al's to Broadway

It started as Al's Pizza in 1969 — a humble little shop on Broadway with a brick oven and a simple idea: make really good pizza, charge fair prices, treat people like neighbours.

Five and a half decades later, the name changed to Broadway Pizza, the oven got an upgrade, and the suburb around us has grown and changed — but the pizza? Same recipe. Same commitment to quality. Same Nedlands institution.

We've fed generations of families, late-night uni students, sports teams after the game, couples on date night, and locals who just want a reliable, great-tasting pizza. That's what we do.

We're not the fanciest pizza in Perth. We're not trying to be. We're the pizza that's been here, that will be here, and that you can always count on.
